Who am I?

A little about me...I was brought up as an Army brat where I traveled the world with my parents. I lived overseas for much of my childhood, and spent a number of years hoppping from state to state for my dad's deployments. My junior and senior year of high school was spent in Kaiserslautern, Germany where I met my wife, Rachel, and even took web development courses together. We started dating in 2005 and have been together ever since.

My wife and I attended the University of Hawaii where I received my Bachelors of Science in Nursing, and Rachel triple-majored in business. We moved to Florida after graduating and moved to Utah after living in Florida for a year. We fell in love in Utah, and have been here ever since.

I've worked for the University of Utah Health for 5 and 1/2 years where I am currently a Nurse Manager of an inpatient medical-psychiatric unit and supervise 40+ employees. We serve patients that are in acute psychiatric crises with co-occuring medical diagnoses. Through my experience as an assistant nurse manager and a nurse manager, I learned a plethora of transferable skills, such as excellent time management, collaboration with a multidisciplinary team, phenomenal work ethics, having difficult conversations with employees and peers, and great at managing projects.

I have incorporated these transferable skills into my web development work, allowing me to tackle work in systematic approach and meeting the needs of the project.
