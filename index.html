Who am I?

A little about me...I've been a registered nurse for the past 7 years, and I've worked as the Nurse Manager of 5 West, the inpatient medical-psychiatric unit at the University of Utah Health for the past 2 years. In my role, I supervise 40+ employee who provide direct patient care to those patients patients that are in acute psychiatric crises with co-occuring medical diagnoses.

Through my experience as the assistant nurse manager and the nurse manager on 5 West, I learned a plethora of transferable skills, such as excellent time management, collaboration with a multidisciplinary team, phenomenal work ethics, having difficult conversations with employees and peers, and great at managing projects.

Though I love nursing and caring for those in need, I've always had a passion for web design and coding since taking classes in high school. The creative nature of the industry and the joy of creating something out of nothing was more than enough to pique my interests. I couldn't be happier with my career switch, and haven't looked back!

It may not the same as saving someone's life, but the fulfillment of this field has exceded all of my expectations, and I'm excited to I incorporate my transferable skills into my web development work, allowing me to tackle work in systematic approach and meeting the needs of the client or project.
